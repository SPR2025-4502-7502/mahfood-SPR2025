The Best Coffee Shops in the Saint Louis Area

Whether you are in South County or in the city, you can find the perfect coffee shop for you! These are the most exciting and tastiest coffee shops in the Saint Louis area!

Coffee Culture

5532 S Lindbergh Blvd. St. Louis, MO 63123

Coffee Culture has a great variety of espresso drinks along with Lotus energy drinks. The shop also has an assortment of cookie flavors.

(314) 221-3835
Website

Cafe Fleur De Lis

5569 Telegraph Rd. St. Louis, MO 63129

Cafe Fleur De Lis has a cozy interior with a variety of syrup and food options for the perfect morning meal.

(314) 200-8158
Website

Catalyst Coffee Bar

2801 Locust St. St. Louis, MO 63103

Catalyst Coffee Bar has an assortment of coffee and tea and is decorated with art from local artists.

(314) 502-2192

Sump Coffee

3700 S Jefferson Ave. St. Louis, MO 63118

Sump Coffee specializes in light roasted single origin coffee, making it unique to other local coffee shops.
