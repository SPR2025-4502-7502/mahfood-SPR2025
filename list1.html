Top 7 Female Pop Albums From the 2020s

These seven albums represent the future of pop music, and show how female artists put their heart and soul into their music.

Image credit

7. Guts (Spilled)

Olivia Rodrigo

2024

Olivia Rodrigo’s deluxe version of her 2nd studio album "Guts (Spilled)" features the hit singles “vampire,” “get him back!” and “obsessed”.

Image credit

6. Midnights

Taylor Swift

2022

Taylor Swift’s 10th studio album "Midnights" features the hit singles “Lavender Haze,” “Anti-Hero” and “Karma”. Midnights also features a collaboration with Lana Del Rey on the fourth track “Snow on the Beach”.

Image credit

5. Short n' Sweet (Deluxe)

Sabrina Carpenter

2025

Sabrina Carpenter’s deluxe version of her breakout 6th studio album "Short n’ Sweet" features the smash hit singles “Espresso,” “Please Please Please” and "Busy Woman".

Image credit

4. Future Nostalgia

Dua Lipa

2020

Dua Lipa’s sophomore full length album "Future Nostaglia" features the hit singles “Levitating” featuring DaBaby, “Don’t Start Now” and “Physical”. Future Nostaglia also includes a collaboration with Angele on the song “Fever”.

Image credit

3. emails i can't send fwd:

Sabrina Carpenter

2023

Sabrina Carpenter’s deluxe version of her 5th studio album "emails i can’t send fwd:" includes the singles “because i liked a boy,” “Nonsense” and “Feather”.

Image credit

2. The Rise and Fall of a Midwest Princess

Chappell Roan

2023

Chappell Roan’s debut album "The Rise and Fall of a Midwest Princess" includes the singles “Red Wine Supernova,” “HOT TO GO!” and “Pink Pony Club”.

Image credit

1. The Tortued Poets Department: The Anthology

Taylor Swift

2024

Taylor Swift’s 11th studio album "The Tortuted Poets Department: The Anthology" includes the singles “Fortnight” feating Post Malone and “I Can Do It With a Broken Heart”. The album also includes a collaboration with Florence + the Machine on the song “Florida!!!”.
